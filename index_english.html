Phrase match report

SEMrush reveals search queries that include the keyword (phrase) being queried. Use this report to get new keywords for your optimization or AdWords campaign.

Related keywords report

Get all keywords related to the keyword being analyzed. Use this report to find alternative opportunities for your SEO and PPC campaigns.

Organic search results

SEMrush shows you exactly which domains rank for the keyword and on which position on the SERP

Ads copies

SEMrush shows every keyword the domain queried has been ranked for in Google's paid results in the past 12 months, and what their position was. If you click on the box, you can view the ad as it appeared.
